What's Inside?

Avil Milk Image

Our Avil milk is actually a concoction of mashed bananas, roasted avil or rice flakes, milk, and peanuts for an additional crunch. Roasted Avil adds to the uniqueness of the dish, lending a crunchy texture to the drink.


Each ingredient added to the drink is full of vital nutrients required for the body. Fresh bananas daily, premium milk, and handcrafted toppings make every glass special.

The Avil Mates Story

Since 1985


Avil Mates started as a small stall in Kochi — our goal was simple: make avil milk that tastes like home. We pick fresh bananas daily, churn milk in small batches, and craft toppings by hand.


The result: an honest, nostalgic treat with modern flair. We focus on freshness, texture and balance — cool, crunchy and creamy in every spoonful.


Today, we're proud to serve authentic Kerala avil milk to thousands of happy customers across Kochi, keeping the tradition alive while innovating for the future.
